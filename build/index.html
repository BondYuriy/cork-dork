Cork Dork is a wine boutique/restaurant located in the city center. We offer excellent local wines with exquisite gastronomy. A place where the characteristics of wine and food are harmoniously combined with each other and form a real melody for gourmets. Our main concept is to give the guest a good mood and a little information about wine and dishes suitable for them - master classes for guests are provided by our professional Sommelier.
