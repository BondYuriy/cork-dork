Membership Types

  • Variety Membership

    As a Variety Member, you will receive the alternating red and white wines each month.

  • Red Club Membership

    As a Red Club Member, you may choose any wine during a red wine month (an additional charge will apply to Cork Dork's Lux Wines, special release wines, and Ice Wine).

  • White Club Membership

    As a White Club Member, you may choose any wine during a white wine month (an additional charge will apply to Cork Dork's Lux Wines, special release wines, and Ice Wine).

  • Sweet Club Membership

    As a Sweet Club Member, you may choose any fruit, sparkling, romance or holiday wine each month.

  • Gift Membership

    Our Wine Club Memberships make great gifts for friends, family members, loved ones or as a corporate gift. Gift Memberships are available in 3, 6, or 12 month periods.

    In addition to choosing a membership type, you also have the choice of receiving one or two bottles each month. Wines may be picked up at any Cork Dork Winery & Restaurant location or shipped directly to your home or business. Wines are shipped and billed quarterly. An adult 21 years or older must be present to sign for the delivery. Delivery option is available only in select states.

Membership Types

  • Earning Points is Fast, Easy, and Free

    For every 350 points earned, Members will receive a $25 Reward to be used at any Cork Dork location. Read more the full rewards terms.

  • Membership Pricing

    Wine Club Members receive a 10%* discount on retail wines sold at Cork Dork Winery & Restaurant (1-5 bottles), a 15%* discount when purchasing a half case or more (6-11 bottles), and a 20%* discount when purchasing a case (any 12 bottles) or more. Members also receive a 10% discount on all carryout orders and a 10% discount on featured retail products.

  • Exclusive Promotions

    Enjoy exclusive promotions for Wine Club Members such as limited bottling offers and other special incentives.

  • Birthday Offers

    Wine Club Members and spouses also receive a complimentary entrée during their birthday month for being part of the club (value up to $15).

  • Monthly Tastings

    Included in the 2-bottle Membership is a monthly tasting experience for two in our tasting room (a $16 value).

  • Members-Only Events

    Wine Club Members are allowed access to Members-Only parties, classes, domestic and international wine trips, and events.

  • Read the terms and conditions.

With Member Signature, we'll charge the card already on file for your account, so all you need to do is provide your Member card or ID, sign the Member Signature section at the bottom of your check, and you'll be on your way!
